This project was originally created by Lumi to give people a shared space to connect and discover sites or games when bored. What was meant to be a simple, ordinary chat app evolved into a full operating system to make the experience more engaging and functional.

The vision for this space began as a spark four years ago, took its first steps in Google Docs two years later, and has now officially come to life as a website for you to enjoy. Welcome to the system.
